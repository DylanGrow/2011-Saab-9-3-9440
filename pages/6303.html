Motor, Tilt Adjustment, Electrically Operated Seat (357B), CV



Motor, Tilt Adjustment, Electrically Operated Seat (357b), CV

Location

Motor, tilt adjustment, electrically operated seat (357b)






Main task

To change the angle of the seat.

Type

DC motor that through a gear drives the mechanism that raises or lowers the seat frame at the front to change the angle of the seat.

Connect

The motor is connected to the switch (357) pins 12 and 7. Depending on the switch position, the motor is powered and grounded via the switch unit. The polarity of the outputs is reversed to change the direction of the motor.
