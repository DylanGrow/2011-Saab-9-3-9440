Fluid Type Specifications



Engine oil

Petrol engine (turbo): Saab Turbo Oil Long Life 0W-30, Mobil 1 0W-40 or fully synthetic engine oil with viscosity 0W-30 or 0W-40 that meets quality requirement GM-LL-A-025.

Saab advises against the use of additional additives.
