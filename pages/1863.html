Soft Top Cover, Moulding



Soft Top Cover, Moulding

To remove

1. Open the soft top cover.
2. Remove the quick nuts from the soft top cover trim. Pull the trim from the velcro fasteners.






3. Remove the soft top cover moulding by unscrewing the nuts and lifting away the moulding.






To fit

1. Fit the soft top cover moulding.
2. Fit the nuts.






3. Position the soft top cover trim over the studs. Fit the nuts. Press the velcro in place.






4. Close the soft top cover.
