Intermediate Service Prior to 20,000 Miles, US/CA



Intermediate service prior to 20,000 miles, US/CA

See Technical description, Service programme. Technical Description, Service Programme

1. Fit protection.

2. Brake fluid - level and condition - check/adjust.

3. Engine oil and oil filter - change

4. Oil and fluid leakage and damage - check.
4WD: Transfer case, differential clutch, limited slip differential, limited slip differential clutch, Rear drive shafts incl. gaiters, Propeller shaft incl. gaiters.

5. Tires - check tread depth and check/adjust pressure.

6. Shift wheels

7. Battery - check the condition.

8. Washer and wiper system, check - check and top up with washer fluid.

9. Service indicator - reset.

10. Road test

11. At the first service: Programme the customer functions.

12. Remove the protection.
