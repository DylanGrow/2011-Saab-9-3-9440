30 31 143 Fresh Air System



30 31 143 Fresh Air System






Specifications

Fresh air system 3M 06970 for breathing protection masks 30 31 127 and 30 31 135. Contains belt and hose package. Supplied without breathing protection mask and equipment for cleaning inlet air.

Range of application

For work with isocyanate products and with products where personal protective equipment is required. See chapter Introduction, Accident prevention measures.

Packaging

1

Transport

No restrictions apply when transporting this product.

Directives

The inlet air for the breathing mask must be cleaned.
