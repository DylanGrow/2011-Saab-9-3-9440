Exhaust System Assembly, B207, US/CA

Exhaust system assembly, B207, US/CA

To remove

1. Raise the car.
2. Undo the joint clamp (A).





3. Remove the exhaust system assembly (B).

To fit

1. Fit the exhaust system assembly (B) with new rubber mountings.





2. Tighten the joint clamp (A).

Tightening torque 40 Nm (30 lbf ft).

3. Lower the car.
4. Connect an exhaust hose and start the engine. Check the exhaust system for leaks.
