Suspension Spring ( Coil / Leaf ): Application and ID

Springs

The springs on the left and right-hand sides of the same axle must be of the same classification in order for the car to maintain the same height on both sides. A section of the chassis data plate, see Springs and dampers, contains information on the type of springs fitted on the car during production. The coded information indicates spring classification.

See the EPC for selection of the correct spare part.
