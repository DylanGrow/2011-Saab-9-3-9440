Floor Console, With UHP Connection



Floor Console, With UHP Connection

To remove

1. Remove the floor console:
- Move the front seats to their rear position.
- Remove the forward side panels (A).







- Move the front seats to their front position.
- Remove the rear cover panel (B) by pulling it out and back.







- Lift the hook (C) and remove the rear side panels by pulling them out and back.







- Move the front seats to their rear position.
- Remove the cover (D), use 82 93 474 Removal tool in the rear edge of the cover and pull back.







- Man: Remove the gear lever gaiter from the cover.
- Aut: Remove the cradle (F).







- Apply the handbrake and remove the cover (G) under the handbrake by pulling the cover straight out.







- Remove the cover on the passenger side by clamping the cover together and lifting up.
- Remove the box under the passenger side's cover (H).
- Remove Phone cradle, UHP.
- Remove the rubber mat in the floor console's storage box. Remove the floor console's screws (I).

NOTE: There are different screws for plastic and aluminium.







- Aut: Place the selector lever in position N.
- Unhook the air duct from the floor console's rear mounting.
- Remove the cable tie (J) from the floor console.







- Unplug the connectors (K) from the ignition switch and the 12 V socket. Remove the cable ties (L).







- Lift the console up and back.

To fit

1. Fit the floor console:
- Position the floor console.
- Plug in the connectors (K) for the ignition switch and the 12 V socket. Fit the cable ties (L).







- Fit the cable tie (J) for the floor console.







- Hook the air duct in the floor console's rear mounting.
- Fit the floor console's screws (I) and fit the rubber mat in the floor console's storage box.

NOTE: There are different screws for plastic and aluminium.







- Fit Phone cradle, UHP (375c).
- Fit the box under the passenger side's cover and fit the cover (H).







- Fit the cover (G) under the handbrake.
- Aut: Fit the cradle (F) and the cover (D).











- Man: Fit the gear lever gaiter and the cover.
- Fit the rear side panels (C).







- Fit the rear cover (B).







- Fit the forward side panels (A).
