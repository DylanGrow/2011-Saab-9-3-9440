1. Fit protection.

2. Lighting - check and adjust.

3. Coolant level and freezing point - check/adjust.

4. Brake fluid, level and condition - check/adjust.

9. Track-rod ends - check the bushings.

10. Exhaust system - inspect the mountings and check for leaks.

11. Fuel tank and fuel lines - check/adjust.

12. Brake pipes and hoses - check/adjust.

13. Brake pads - check.

14. Shift wheels.

15. Handbrake - check the function.

16. Tires - check tread depth and check/adjust pressure.

17. Fresh air filter - change and cleaning drainage hose.

18. Washer and wiper system, check and top up with washer fluid.

19. Locks, door restraints and hinges - check/lubricate.

20. Safety belts - check/adjust.

21. Airbag system - check.

22. Service indicator - reset.

23. Road test.
