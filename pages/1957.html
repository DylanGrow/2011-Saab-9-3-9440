Belt Reel Inner Cover, Front Seat, CV



Belt Reel Inner Cover, Front Seat, CV

To remove

1. Remove the seat trim and cushion.
2. Remove the plugs for the belt reel inner cover.






3. Remove the rear screw for the belt reel inner cover.
4. Remove the front screws for the belt reel inner cover.






5. Remove the cable tie holding the lumbar support pump and move the pump aside.
6. Fold the backrest forward and lift the cover at the back.

To fit

1. Position the inner cover and fit the front screws.






2. Fit the rear screws for the belt reel inner cover.






3. Fit the plugs for the belt reel inner cover.
4. Fit the lumbar support pump.
5. Fit seat cushion and upholstery.
