Cooling Spray, Frigi-Test Oxyd-Ex



Cooling Spray, Frigi-Test Oxyd-Ex






Specifications

Frigi-Test Oxyd-Ex. 250 ml bottle.

Range of application

For example, checking of electrically heated seats.

Transport

The product is classified as dangerous goods when transporting. Special regulations apply to packaging, marking and transport, therefore existing lead times cannot be guaranteed.

Directives

The product is not flammable. There is danger of frostbite in case of skin contact. Pressurised can. Must not be exposed to direct sunlight or temperatures above +50 °C. Must not be punctured or incinerated. Also applies to empty can. Must be stored separately from materials which may ignite the product - Smoking is prohibited. Make sure that ventilation is adequate. Avoid skin and eye contact.
