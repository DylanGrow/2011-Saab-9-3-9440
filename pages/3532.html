Bootlid Lock Microswitch (622)



Bootlid Lock Microswitch (622)

Location

Bootlid lock microswitch (622)

Main task

Used to open the bootlid from the outside.

Type






The switch contains a single pole breaker. When the handle is not touched the breaker is open. When the handle is pushed in the switch makes.

Connect






The control module input is digital. A voltage above 2V is interpreted as an open circuit while lower than 2V is interpreted as closed.
