Safety-Related Torque Limitation, 7x



Safety-related Torque Limitation, 7x

General
The following function can limit engine torque for safety reasons when there is a fault in the throttle control.






- Max throttle when pedal released (70)

Safety-related torque limitation
Maximum engine torque can be limited in case of a fault in the throttle control. Depending on the type of fault, the limit can be set to 170 Nm or 140 Nm.
