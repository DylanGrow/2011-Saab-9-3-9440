Roof Rail, 5D



Roof Rail, 5D

To remove

1. Remove the plastic covers on the roof rail.







2. Remove the screws on the roof rail.
3. Lift off the roof rail.

To fit

1. Put in place the roof rail.







2. Fit the screws on the roof rail.

Tightening torque: 12 Nm (9 lbf ft)

3. Fit the plastic covers on the roof rail.
