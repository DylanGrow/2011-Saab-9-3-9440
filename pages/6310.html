Backrest Rake Motor



Backrest Rake Motor

Removing

1. Remove the seat.
2. Remove front seat side panel.
3. Remove the cover on the same side as the seatbelt buckle.
4. Unplug the motor.
5. Remove the upper and lower clip from the motor.






6. Pull the backrest rake motor straight up.

Fitting

1. Fit the backrest rake motor. Hold the opposite side of the shaft when fitting the motor and upper clip.






2. Fit the lower clip to the motor.
3. Plug in the backrest rake motor.
4. Fit the cover on the same side as the seatbelt buckle.
5. Fit front seat side panel.
6. Fit the seat.
7. Test run the backrest adjustment motor.
