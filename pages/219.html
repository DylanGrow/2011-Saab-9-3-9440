110000 MI or 176000 KM

Reset
    Service Reminder Indicators
Diagnose/Test
    Vehicle
      Road Test: Check the following. Ignition switch: gear-lever lock operates. Check the parking position lock on cars with automatic transmission. Lighting: Check front & rear indicator, brake, turn, headlamp, front/rear fog, lic plate, interior, instrument warning/indicator. Engine: function and noise level. Clutch: operation. Manual gearbox: operation. Automatic transmission: starting interlock, noise, shift performance. Wheels: balance and out of roundness. Steering: steering wheel position, directional stability, operation of power steering. Brakes: pedal travel, handbrake lever travel. Cruise Control: functionality of the system. Heating and ventilation unit: operation system and controls. Wipers and washers: washer jet pattern & wiper operation. Check that the clock shows the right time.
Inspect
    Wiper and Washer Systems
      Check wiper system, top up washer fluid.
    Fluids
      Check for oil and fluid leakage, check for damage
    Fluid - Transfer Case
      AWD models only
    Fluid - Differential
      AWD Models only
    Drive/Propeller Shaft
      AWD models only
    Brake Fluid
      Check brake fluid; status and level
    Battery
      Check state of condition with SAAB Battery Tester.
    Air Bag Systems
      Check airbag system, SRS warning lamp, visual inspection
Replace
    Oil Filter, Engine
    Engine Oil
Rotate
    Tires
      Rotate tires, check tire pressure, tread depth and wear
