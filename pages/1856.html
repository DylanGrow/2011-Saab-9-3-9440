Third Bow



Third Bow

Removing

1. Remove the Headlining until the headlining plastic holder can be removed from the third bow.
2. Detach the outer roof from the third bow.






3. Lift off the bow mounting locking lugs using two screwdrivers. Knock loose the bow together with the bow mounting.
4. Remove the third bow.

Fitting

1. Put the third bow in mounting position. Press so that the bow mounting locking lugs hook on.






2. Remove the soft top support and raise the soft top fully.
3. Affix new double-sided adhesive tape to the third bow. Fold the fabric tabs of the outer roof around the third bow. Observe the centre markings on the parts.






4. Operate the soft top half way down and secure it with the soft top support to prevent it falling down when the hydraulic pressure drops.






5. Fit the Headlining.
