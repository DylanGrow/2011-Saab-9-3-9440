All Window Lifts Out Of Order



All Window Lifts Out Of Order

Fault symptom

All window lifts out of order

No windows move.

Conditions

No DTCs stored with the fault symptom in question.

Diagnostic help

In order that the window lifts shall work the system voltage must be between 11.5 and 15.5 V.

Check the condition of the battery.

Check the battery charger voltage with trickle charging.
