Floor Carpet, Rear



Floor Carpet, Rear

To remove

1. Remove the rear seat cushion.
2. Remove the front seats.
3. Remove the floor console.
4. Remove the rear air duct for the rear panel vent.
5. Detach the handbrake cables.
6. Remove the scuff plates.
7. Detach the lower edge of the B-pillar trim.
8. Remove the rear screws from the centre console.
9. Remove the forward screws from the centre console.
10. Lift up the aluminium frame of the centre console so as to release the carpet.
11. Detach the carpet from the B-pillar trim.
12. Pull the carpet backwards. Ensure that the cables and mounting screws do not damage the carpet.

To fit

1. Position the rear carpet. Ensure that the carpet is correctly located under the B-pillar trim and under the centre console frame.
2. Reposition the aluminium frame of the centre console.
3. Fit the forward screws of the centre console.
4. Fit the rear screws of the centre console.
5. Attach the lower trim on the B-pillars.
6. Fit the scuff plates.
7. Attach the handbrake cables.
8. Fit the air duct leading to rear panel vent.
9. Fit the floor console.
10. Fit the front seats.
11. Fit the rear seat cushion.
