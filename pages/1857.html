Fourth Bow



Fourth Bow

Removing

1. Remove the Headlining until the headlining plastic holder can be removed from the fourth bow.
2. Detach the outer roof from the fourth bow.






3. Remove the seal by pulling it out of the middle part of the U-shaped rail. Unhook the upper part of the seal from the fastener. Let the seal hang.






4. Unhook the outer roof attaching profiles from the rear rails.






5. Remove the hydraulic line mounting from the right-hand side of the fourth bow.






6. Drill out the blind rivets for the fourth bow guide strap.
7. Remove the reinforcing bow bolts on one side.
8. Remove the retaining clips and the fourth bow.






Fitting

1. Place the fourth bow in mounting position. Fit the retaining clips.






2. Put the reinforcing bow and the bow's guide straps in mounting position. Fit the bolts.
3. Fit the blind rivets to the fourth bow guide strap.






4. Fit the mounting to the hydraulic lines on the right-hand side of the fourth bow.
5. Hook the outer roof attaching profiles to the rear rails.






6. Fit the rear rail seals. Hook the top part of the seal to the mounting and then press it into the rail






7. Remove the soft top support and raise the soft top fully.
8. Affix new double-sided adhesive tape to the fourth bow. Fold the fabric tabs of the outer roof around the fourth bow. Observe the centre markings on the parts.






9. Operate the soft top half way down and secure it with the soft top support to prevent it falling down when the hydraulic pressure drops.






10. Fit the Headlining.
