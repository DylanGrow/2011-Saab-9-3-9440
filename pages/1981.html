Side Panel, Electric Front Seat, CV



Side Panel, Electric Front Seat, CV

To remove

1. Move seat to its highest position.
2. Remove the side trim screw. Remove the side trim with switch unit by pulling forward the trim.






3. Disconnect the connector from the switch units.






To fit

1. Attach the connector to the switch units.






2. Fit the cover, ensuring that all fixings are properly located and locked. Fit the screw.
