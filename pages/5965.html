Scuff Plate, Boot Lid



Scuff Plate, Boot Lid

To remove

1. Open the boot lid and lift up the luggage compartment mat.
2. Detach the scuff plate clips.







3. Remove the scuff plate by pulling it straight up so that the clips release.

To fit

1. Lift up the luggage compartment mat. Refit the scuff plate by guiding the clips into the sill holes. Press the scuff plate into place.







2. Fit the clips and reposition the luggage compartment mat.
3. Check the fit and close the boot lid.
