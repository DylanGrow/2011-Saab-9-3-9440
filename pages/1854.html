Reinforcing Bow



Reinforcing Bow

NOTE:
- The reinforcing bow is located between the third and fourth bow.
- This is not counted as a bow.

Removing

1. Operate the soft top half way down and secure it with 82 93 847 Soft top support to prevent it falling down when the hydraulic pressure drops.






2. Remove the seal by pulling it out of the middle part of the U-shaped rail. Unhook the upper part of the seal from the fastener. Let the seal hang.






3. Unhook the outer roof attaching profiles from the rear rails.






4. Remove the mounting for the hydraulic lines from the right-hand side of the reinforcing bow.






5. Remove the reinforcing bow bolts on the rear rails. Lift out the reinforcing bow.






Fitting

1. Insert the reinforcing bow between the insulation of the outer roof and the headlining.
2. Put the reinforcing bow and the bows' guide straps in mounting position. Fit the bolts.
Tightening torque: 10 Nm






3. Fit the hydraulic line holder on the right-hand side of the reinforcing bow.






4. Hook the outer roof attaching profiles to the rear rails.






5. Fit the rear rail seals. Hook the top part of the seal to the mounting and then press it into the rail






6. Remove the soft top support and raise the soft top.
