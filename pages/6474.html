Sound Absorbent Panel, Rear Wheel Housing



Sound Absorbent Panel, Rear Wheel Housing

To remove

1. Remove the rear seat back rest.







2. Lift off the floor panel from the centre mounting of the rear back rest. Lift out the floor panel.







3. Remove the 3 two-stage rivets from the parcel shelf.







4. Remove the Side bolsters.
5. Remove the clips which attach the luggage compartment inspection cover using special tool 84 71 179 removal tool, clips and remove the inspection cover.







6. Remove the two-stage rivets and the two clips which attach the luggage compartment side trim. Use the 84 71 179 removal tool, clips. Remove the side trim.







7. Using 84 71 179 removal tool, clips remove the cable clips holding the cabling to the sound absorbent panel and push aside the cabling.







8. Remove the two plugs holding the sound absorbent panel.
9. Remove the sound absorbent panel.

To fit

1. Fit the sound absorbent panel with the two plugs.







2. Fit the cables to the sound absorbent panel with the cable clips.
3. Fit the luggage compartment side trim with the two-stage rivets and the two clips.







4. Fit the luggage compartment inspection cover with the clips.







5. Fit the Side bolsters.







6. Fit the 3 two-stage rivets on the parcel shelf.
7. Lift in the floor panel. Make sure that the floor panel is correctly located over the centre mounting of the rear back rest.







8. Fit the rear seat back rest.
