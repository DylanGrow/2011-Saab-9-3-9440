Solenoid Valve, Turbo By-Pass (605), B207



Solenoid Valve, Turbo By-pass (605), B207

To remove
1. Remove the upper engine cover.






2. Remove the connector from the solenoid valve.






3. Press down the catch carefully with a screwdriver and remove the solenoid valve from its mounting.
4. Detach the hoses from the solenoid valve.

To fit
1. Connect the hoses to the solenoid valve.






a. To inlet pipe
b. To bypass valve
c. To temp/pressure on turbocharger delivery pipe

2. Fit the solenoid valve to the mounting, make sure the hoses are not pinched together and adjust them as necessary.
3. Attach the connector.
4. Replace the upper engine cover.
