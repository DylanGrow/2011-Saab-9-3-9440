Luggage Compartment Trim, Floor Panel, 4D



Luggage Compartment Trim, Floor Panel, 4D

To remove

1. Remove the rear seat back rest.







2. Lift off the floor panel from the centre mounting of the rear back rests.







3. Lift out the floor panel.







To fit

1. Position the floor panel.







2. Make sure that the floor panel is correctly located over the centre mounting of the rear back rests.
3. Fit the rear seat back rest.
