Bootlid Gas Cartridge Spring, CV



Bootlid Gas Cartridge Spring, CV

To remove

1. Open the boot lid.
2. Remove the clips from the ball fitting.






3. Remove the gas cartridge spring from the ball stud.

To fit

1. Place the gas cartridge spring in position and press the top and bottom firmly in place on the ball stud.
