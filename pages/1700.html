Interior Moulding / Trim: Description and Operation



Brief Description

Door trim

The door trim has an injection-moulded carrier, with TPO foil and textile or leather/vinyl inserts. There are 3 types of decor strip: wood, plastic or mat chrome.

Headlining

The headlining is made of cellular plastic with a textile cover. The headlining insulates against heat, cold and noise. The headlining has energy-absorbent material and retainers for the wiring harness.

Carpets

The floor carpet consists of a front and rear carpet which is of a textile type and extends up to the sills.

Dashboard

The dashboard is based on a 3 mm thick plastic frame that is covered with foil. Polyurethane foam is injected between the frame and the foil, providing a soft and absorbing surface. The dashboard is shaped so that the driver can easily reach all controls and switches. The opening button for the glove compartment is integrated in the decor strip on the dashboard.

Glove box

The glove box lid is strengthened with a steel plate and serves as protection for the passenger's knees in the event of a collision.

Cars with ACC have a hose connecting the evaporator housing and glove compartment. Furthest in in the glove compartment is a sliding vent that can be opened to cool the compartment. A lamp illuminates the glove compartment when it is open.
