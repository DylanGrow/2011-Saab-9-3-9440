90 297 970 Flange Sealant



90 297 970 Flange Sealant






Specifications

Loctite 518. Replaces cut gaskets, fast hardening. 50 g.

Range of application

All manual gearboxes (not 6-speed).

Packaging

1

Transport

No restrictions apply when transporting this product.

Directives

The product is irritable to the respiratory system and skin. Risk of serious eye injury. Avoid inhaling fumes. In case of contact with the eyes, rinse immediately with plenty of water and seek medical help. In case of contact with skin, wash immediately with plenty of soap and water. Use protective goggles or face shield. Provide adequate ventilation. Avoid contact with skin and eyes. Contains 2- hydroxyethyl metacrylate, 1-acetyl 2-phenylhydracine. Can cause allergic reactions.
