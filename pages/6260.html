Rear Drainage Hose, Sunroof 4D



Rear Drainage Hose, Sunroof 4D

NOTE: Pay attention to cleanliness so that the headlining does not become dirty.

To remove

1. Remove the headlining, see Headlining, 4D. Headlining, 4D, US/CA.
2. Remove the inspection cover.
- Right-hand side:
- Remove the clip (A) and fold down the inspection cover.






- Remove the clip (B) that holds the wiring harness to the rear lamp






- Remove the insulation, 2 clips (C).
- Left-hand side:
- Open the inspection cover.






- Remove the amplifier unit for the radio. See Amplifier.
- Remove the insulation. 3 clips (A)






3. Remove the rear drainage hose.
- Remove the hose (A) from the sunroof assembly.






- Remove the hose from the clips, right-hand side (A)






- Remove the hose from the clips, left-hand side (A)






- Remove the hose from the sound trap (B) behind the wheel housing in the luggage compartment.






- Pull the hose out from the cabin.

To fit

1. Fitting the rear drainage hose.
- Route a new hose through the hole down to the sound trap at the wheel housing.
- Secure the hose to the sound trap (B) behind the wheel housing in the luggage compartment.






- Secure the hose in the clips, right-hand side (A).






- Secure the hose in the clips, left-hand side (A).






- Secure the hose (A) for the sunroof assembly.






2. Fit the inspection cover.
- Right-hand side:
- Refit the insulation. 2 clips (A)






- Fit the clip (B) that holds the wiring harness to the rear lamp.
- Fold up the inspection cover and fit the clip (A).






- Left-hand side:
- Refit the insulation. 3 clips (A).






- Fit the amplifier unit for the radio. See Amplifier.
- Fold back the inspection cover.






3. Fit the headlining, see Headlining, 4D. Headlining, 4D, US/CA.
