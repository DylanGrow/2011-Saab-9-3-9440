Electronic Throttle Actuator: Service and Repair



Actuator, Throttle Body (604), B207

To remove
1. Remove the upper engine cover.






2. Detach the turbocharger delivery hose from the throttle body and bend it aside.






3. Carefully bend up the bracket catch and remove the EVAP canister purge valve from the bracket.






4. Remove the bracket from the throttle body and carefully bend it aside.
5. Unplug the connector from the throttle body.
6. Remove the throttle body.
7. Remove the old seal and clean the sealing surfaces.

To fit
1. Fit the throttle body with a new seal.






2. Attach the connector.
3. Fit the bracket.
4. Fit the EVAP canister purge valve and bend the catch back in place.
5. Fit the turbocharger delivery hose to the throttle body.






6. Fit the upper engine cover.
