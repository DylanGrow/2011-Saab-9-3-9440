Air Bag Deactivation Switch: Service and Repair



Switch, Passenger Airbag

To remove

1. Remove the cover (A).






2. Detach the electrical connection (A).






3. Press in the clips (B) and remove the switch.






To fit

1. Fit the switch.






2. Fit the electrical connection (A).






3. Fit the cover (A).
