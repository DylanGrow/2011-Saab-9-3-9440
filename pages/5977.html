Luggage Compartment Front Trim, CV



Luggage Compartment Front Trim, CV

To remove

1. Open the boot lid and remove the luggage compartment floor trim.
2. Remove the rivets and lift out the front section of the luggage compartment trim.







To fit

1. Fit the front section of the luggage compartment trim with the rivets.







2. Fit the luggage compartment trim into place.
3. Close the boot lid.
