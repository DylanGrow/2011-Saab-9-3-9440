Control Module, Sunroof (182)



Control Module, Sunroof (182)

Location

Control module, sunroof (182).

Main task

Handle open and closing and logic for the sunroof

Type

Combined with motor

Power supply, ground and bus communication

The control module with motor is fed with B+ on pin 5 on the control module, grounding to pin 6 and pin 2 on the control module. Control signal to pins 3 and 4. Bus communication takes place from pin 1.
