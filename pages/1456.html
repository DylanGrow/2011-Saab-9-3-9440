Safety And Handling Instructions, PPS

General safety instructions, PPS

WARNING:
The following points must be followed precisely when working in the PPS system.
- All parts included in the spare parts kit are calibrated to one another. All of the parts must therefore be installed on the seat in order for the PPS system to be correctly calibrated.
- All cars are equipped with an airbag system that has side airbags as a component. The side airbag is fitted under the backrest upholstery on each front seat.
- Work in accordance with the Safety and handling regulations.
