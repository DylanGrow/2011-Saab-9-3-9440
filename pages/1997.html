Upholstery, Rear Backrest, 4D



Upholstery, Rear Backrest, 4D

Removing

1. Remove the 60% part of the backrest from the rear seat.
2. Remove the head restraint supports.






3. Remove the armrest from the backrest 60% part.
4. Remove the backrest belt guide.
5. Remove the sprung backrest catch.
6. Remove the through-load hatch frame.






7. Remove the upholstery strips from the backrest.
8. Remove the through-load hatch by undoing the locating pins at the lower edge of the backrest.
9. Carefully pull the upholstery off the foam backrest cushion.






Fitting

1. Fit the upholstery to the foam backrest cushion.






2. Fit the through-load hatch.
3. Fit the backrest upholstery strips. Make sure the upholstery is properly stretched.






4. Fit the through-load hatch frame.
5. Fit the sprung backrest catch.
6. Fit the seatbelt guide to the backrest.






7. Fit the armrest to the backrest 60% part.
8. Fit the head restraint supports and head restraint. Ensure that head restraint support catches engage properly.
9. Fit the backrest 60% part to the rear seat.
