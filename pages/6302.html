Motor, Tilt Adjustment, Electrically Operated Driver's Seat With Memory (357DB)



Motor, Tilt Adjustment, Electrically Operated Driver's Seat With Memory (357Db)

Location

Motor, tilt adjustment, electrically operated driver's seat with memory (357Db)

4D/5D






CV






Main task

To change the angle of the seat.


Type

DC motor that through a gear drives the mechanism that raises or lowers the seat frame at the front to change the angle of the seat.

Connect

The motor is connected to the seat control module (357Dk) pin 60 and 57. The information on the required rake adjustment comes from the switch (357D) to the control module. The control module uses the information from the switch to supply power to and ground the motor. The outputs are pole-reversed to change the direction of rotation of the motor.
