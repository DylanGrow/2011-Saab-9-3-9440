93 160 535 Adhesive Kit, Body Repair



93 160 535 Adhesive Kit, Body Repair

Specifications

Two-component
- Component A (resin) 122 ml
Component B (hardener) 73 ml

- Static mixer, screwed on (white)
- Cleaning solution - 100 ml 3x
- Cleaning cloth (green) 3x
- Drying cloth (blue) 3x
- Work instructions

Range of application

For body repairs

Packaging

1

Transport

The product is classified as dangerous goods when transporting. Special regulations apply to packaging, marking and transport, therefore existing lead times cannot be guaranteed.

Directives

Cleaning solution

This product is extremely flammable and causes irritation. Irritates the eyes. Could cause allergy in the event of skin contact. Fumes can make you drowsy and dazed. Keep container tightly closed. Keep away from sources of ignition - No smoking. Avoid inhaling fumes. In the event of contact with eyes, rinse immediately with plenty of water and seek medical advice. This material and its container must be disposed of in a safe way. If swallowed, seek medical advice immediately and show this container or the label. Wear suitable protective clothing and gloves.

Component A (resin)

May cause sensitisation by skin contact. Irritates the eyes and skin. Toxic to aquatic organisms, could cause long-term adverse effects in the aquatic environment. Avoid contact with the skin. In the event of contact with eyes, rinse immediately with plenty of water and seek medical advice. In the event of contact with skin, wash immediately with plenty of soap and water. If swallowed, seek medical advice immediately and show this container or the label. Avoid discharge into the environment. Refer to special instructions/product information sheets. This material and its container must be disposed of as hazardous waste. Wear suitable protective clothing, gloves and eye/face protection.

Component B (hardener)

Corrosive. May cause sensitisation by skin contact. Hazardous in the event of skin contact and swallowing. Toxic to aquatic organisms, could cause long-term adverse effects in the aquatic environment. In the event of contact with eyes, rinse immediately with plenty of water and seek medical advice. In the event of contact with skin, wash immediately with plenty of soap and water. In the event of an accident or if you feel unwell, seek medical advice immediately (show the label where possible). Avoid discharge into the environment. Wear suitable protective clothing, gloves and eye/face protection.
