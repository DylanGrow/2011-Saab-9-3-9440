Procedures



Sunroof, (Glass Hatch), Adjustment

Adjustment

1. Move the sun visor back and open the sunroof at the rear edge.
2. Cars with side protection: Carefully remove the glass hatch side protection (A) from the clips in the front and rear edge on both sides. Close the sunroof.






3. Cars with side protection: Move the side protection to the side.
4. Adjust the glass hatch's retaining bolts (B) at the front and rear edge so that the edges are level with the edge of the roof.






IMPORTANT: The front of the hatch must not be too high.

5. Tighten the glass hatch's retaining bolts (B).
6. Cars with side protection: Open the sunroof at the rear edge. Fit the side protection for the glass hatch to the clips (A).
7. Check the function.
