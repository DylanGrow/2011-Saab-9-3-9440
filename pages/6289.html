CV



Motor, Backrest Adjustment, Electrically Operated Seat (357d), CV

Location

Motor, backrest adjustment, electrically operated seat (357d)






Main task

To change the angle of the backrest.

Type

DC motor that via a gear drives the mechanism that controls the reclining angle of the backrest.

Connect

The motor is connected to the switch unit pins 5 and 11, depending on the switch's position, the motor is voltage fed and ground via the switch unit. The polarity of the outputs are reversed to change the motor's direction of rotation.
