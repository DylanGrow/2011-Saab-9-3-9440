Rear Suspension

Dampers

The chassis plate section Springs and dampers contains information on the type of damper fitted on the car during production. This coded information indicates damper classification.

Note
Left and right front dampers are separate parts with different part numbers. However, the code is the same for both dampers.

See the EPC for selection of the correct spare part.
