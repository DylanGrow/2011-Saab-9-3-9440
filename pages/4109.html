Wheel Bolt, Replacing

Wheel bolt, replacing

Note
It is not necessary to remove the wheel when replacing one or more wheel bolts.

If all wheel bolts are to be replaced, replace one at a time and torque tighten as follows.





Aluminum wheel

Oil the bolt threads.

Tightening torque: 110 Nm (81 lbf ft)

Pressed steel wheels

Tightening torque: 50 Nm +90° +90°, max 110 Nm (37 lbf ft +90° +90°, max 81 lbf ft)

Note
To avoid tightening the bolts too hard when fitting steel wheels, angle tighten with a torque wrench set to 110 Nm (81 lbf ft). If the torque wrench indicates that 110 Nm (81 lbf ft) has been reached, stop tightening.
