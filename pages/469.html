Limited Performance



Limited Performance

The SVS lamp illuminates when a fault has arisen in the engine or gearbox (cars with automatic gearbox) which could result in driveability problems but which do not affect the exhaust emission control.

The car can still be driven but perhaps with limited performance. Contact a workshop at a suitable time.

We recommend that you contact an authorized Saab workshop.
