Rear Silencer

Rear silencer

To remove

1. Raise the car.
2. Cut the exhaust pipe to dimension, 100 mm. Cut it at right-angles using 83 95 667 Pipe cutterhaust system.





3. Remove the rear silencer.

To fit

1. Clean the cut to remove burrs and dirt.
2. Fit the joint clamp on the pipe.





3. Fit the rear silencer with new rubber mountings.
4. Change the rubber mountings for the front silencer as well if necessary.
5. Adjust the joint clamp so that the pipe joint is in the middle of the clamp.
6. Adjust the front and rear silencers so they balance.
7. On variants with visible tail pipe: Make sure the tail pipe is positioned centrally in the recess in the rear bumper and follows the profile of the bumper.
8. Tighten the clamp nuts.

Tightening torque 40 Nm (30 lbf ft).

9. Lower the car.
10. Connect an exhaust hose and start the engine. Check that the exhaust does not leak.
