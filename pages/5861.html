Rear Door, Inner Door Pull



Rear Door, Inner Door Pull

To remove

1. Remove Cover, rear switch, window lift.
2. Remove the door pull.
- Press the door pull out from its snap mountings.






- Pull out the handle's upper mounting






- Then turn the handle backwards/out from the trim.

To fit

1. Fit the door pull.
- Insert the handle and check that the door pull engages in its mountings.






2. Fit Cover, rear switch, window lift.
