83 95 931 Engine Oil



83 95 931 Engine Oil






Specifications

Synthetic based oil Super Blend SAE 5W-30. Lowest flow point: -39 °C. Grade API SJ, ACEA A1/B1- 98 and ILSAC GF-2. 208 l drum.

Saab application

For petrol engines in:
- Saab 900
- Saab 9000
- Saab 9-3 (not 9-3 (9440))
- Saab 9-5 -M03
