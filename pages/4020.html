Power Steering Fluid Level

Power steering fluid level





1. Open the cap and check the level on the dipstick.
2. If fluid is low, investigate the cause.
3. Top up with power steering fluid as necessary.

Warning
Exercise caution with oil spills on hot surfaces. Risk of fire.
