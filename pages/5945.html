Floor Carpet, Rear, CV



Floor Carpet, Rear, CV

To remove

1. Remove the front seats.
2. Remove the rear seat cushion.
3 Remove the floor console.
4. Remove the rear air duct for the rear panel vent from the centre console.
5. Unhook the hand brake cables.
6. Remove the doors' scuff plates.
7. Remove the rear screws from the centre console.







8. Remove the forward screws from the centre console.
9. Pull up the handbrake lever fully.
10. Remove the grounding cable under the centre console.







11. Remove the ACM rear connector.
12. Cut the carpet along the marked arrows.







13. Lift up the aluminium frame of the centre console so as to release the carpet.
14. Free the carpet from the floor vents.
15. Pull the carpet backwards and lift it out. Ensure that the cables and mounting screws do not damage the carpet.

To fit

1. Lift in and position the rear carpet. Make sure that the carpet is under the centre console aluminium frame.
2. Align the front and rear carpets.
3. Tape the carpet underneath along the line of the edge. LINK Use tape, type no. 30 04 785.







4. Connect the ACM rear connector.







5. Connect the grounding cable under the centre console.
6. Fit the forward screws of the centre console.







7 Fit the rear screws of the centre console.
8 Fit the doors' scuff plates.
9. Attach the handbrake cables.
10. Fit the rear air duct for the rear panel vent in the centre console.
11. Fit the floor console.
12. Fit the rear seat cushion.
13. Fit the front seats.
