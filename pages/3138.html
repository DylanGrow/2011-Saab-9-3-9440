Chain Grease, Gleitmo 540



Chain Grease, Gleitmo 540

Specifications

Gleitmo 540. Synthetic adhesive grease with molybdenum sulphide. 1 l bottle.

Saab application

For example, for brake caliper sliding surfaces. Saab 900 -M87 (front).

Transport

The product is classified as dangerous goods when transporting. Special regulations apply to packaging, marking and transport, therefore existing lead times cannot be guaranteed.

Directives

The product is flammable. May be hazardous if inhaled after frequent and repeated exposure. May be hazardous in case of prolonged skin contact. Avoid inhaling vapors/mist. Avoid skin contact. Make sure there is adequate ventilation. Must not be poured down the drain.
