Luggage Compartment Trim, CV



Luggage Compartment Trim, CV

To remove

1. Open the bootlid.
2. Remove the floor trim.
3. Remove the scuff plate.







4. Remove the side cover from the appropriate side of the luggage compartment opening.
5. Remove the rivets from the side trim on the appropriate side.

Fold out the trim







6. Left-hand trim: Remove the connector from the lighting
7. Unhook the frame trim.
8. Lift out the side trim.

To fit

1. Set the trim in place. Make sure that the side trim is inside the front trim.
2. Hook back the frame trim

Left-hand trim: Connect the lamp connector.
3. Fit the rivets.







4. Fit the rear side trims with the clips.







5. Fit the scuff plate.
6. Fit the luggage compartment floor trim.
