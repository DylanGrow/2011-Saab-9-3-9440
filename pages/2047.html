Bodywork On Cars With Airbag

All Saab 9-3 Sport Sedan are equipped with an airbag system. Note the following when carrying out body repairs:

- Read the section Work on cars equipped with airbag.
- Read the section Safety and handling instructions before starting work.
- When performing work that subjects the body to blows and jabs, the ignition must be OFF.
- When welding, the negative lead of the battery must be disconnected and covered.
- When painting, the bolt holes of control modules and grounding points must be covered to safeguard the control module's ground contact.

For more detailed information on the airbag system, see Detailed description.
