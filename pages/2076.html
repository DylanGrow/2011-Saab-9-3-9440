Water In the Luggage Compartment



Water In The Luggage Compartment

Fault symptoms

Water in the luggage compartment

Cause: As a result of inadequate sealing there could be leakage in through the area adjacent to the hinge plate following heavy rain or car washing.

Cars affected: 9-3 CV

Materials

90 273 919 Seal
12 794 960 Tape

Procedure

The procedure must be carried out on both sides.

1. Remove the grey tape.
2. Apply the seal 90 273 919 as illustrated.






3. Fit new tape.
