Intermediate Service, Subsequent, US/CA



Intermediate Service, Subsequent, US/CA

See Technical description, Service programme. Technical Description, Service Programme

1. Fit protection.

2. Brake fluid - level and condition - check/adjust.

3. Engine oil and oil filter - replace.

4. Oil and fluid leakage and damage - check.

5. Brake pads - check.

6. Shift wheels.

7. Tires - check tread depth and check/adjust pressure.

8. Battery - check the condition.

9. Washer and wiper system, check and top up with washer fluid.

10. Condition of the drive belt and belt tensioner - check.

11. Safety belts - check/adjust.

12. Airbag components - check.

13. Service indicator - reset.

14. Road test.

15. Remove the protection.

Petrol engine: Spark plugs (157)- replace every 80,000 miles/8 years
B284: Drive belt, auxiliary unit - replace every 150,000 miles
