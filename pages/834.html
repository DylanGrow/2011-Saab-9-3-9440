The Gear Jams or Is Stiff to Engage

The gear jams or is stiff to engage

Fault symptom

The gear jams or is stiff to engage especially in cold temperature

Conditions

When changing from first to second or from third to second, the gear is difficult to engage.

Action

Adjust the gear cables, see Adjusting gear cables. Adjustments
