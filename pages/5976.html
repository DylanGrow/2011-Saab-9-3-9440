C-Pillar Trim, 5D



C-pillar Trim, 5D

To remove

1. Fold down the rear seat backrest.
2. Remove the screw cover from the C-pillar trim. Use 82 93 474 Removal tool.







3. Remove the screw from the C-pillar trim.
4. Remove the C-pillar trim by pulling it up/out to release the clips from the C-pillar.

To fit

1. Fit the C-pillar trim.







2. Fit the screw to the C-pillar trim.
3. Fit the cover on the C-pillar trim.
4. Fold back the rear seat backrest.
