Wiring Harness: Service and Repair



Wiring Harness, Injectors, B207

To remove
1. Remove the upper engine cover (A).






2. Unplug the injectors' connector (A).






3. Unplug the wiring harness' connector from the engine (A).






4. Unplug the connector from the clip under the intake manifold (B).
5. Move up the wiring harness.

To fit
1. Move down the engine's connector under the intake manifold.
2. Fit the connector to the clip under the intake manifold (A).






3. Plug in the wiring harness' connector for the engine (B).
4. Plug in the injectors' connector (A).






5. Fit the upper engine cover (A).
