Lower Rear Spoiler



Lower Rear Spoiler

To remove

1. Raise the car.
2. Remove the centre nuts of the spoiler.







3. Remove the bolts from the outer edges of the spoiler.







4. Begin from the sides and pull the spoiler loose so that the mountings release from the bumper shell.

To fit

1. Use the guide pin to position the spoiler. Press the mountings into the bumper shell holder.







2. Fit the centre nuts of the spoiler.







3. Fit the outer bolts of the spoiler.







4. Lower the car and check the fit of the spoiler.
