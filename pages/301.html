Reddish Engine Oil

Reddish engine oil

Fault symptom

Oil discolored reddish.

Criteria

When checking the oil level, the oil appears discolored. This is due to an oil leak detection agent used in production on a certain percentage of engines.

Procedure

No measures required. Neither the oil nor the engine are faulty as a result.
