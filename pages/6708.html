Dome Lamp: Service and Repair



Interior Lighting, Front, CV

To remove

1. Remove the roof lamp from the windscreen frame trim.







2. Remove the bulb's bayonet fitting.

To fit

1. Fit the bulb in the bulb holder.
2. Fit the bulb holder in the roof lamp.
3. Fit the roof lamp.
