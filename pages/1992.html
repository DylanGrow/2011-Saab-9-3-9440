Rear Seat Backrest Lock, 5D



Rear Seat Backrest Lock, 5D

To remove

1. Lower the rear seat backrest.
2. Remove Backrest lock plastic covers, 5D.
3. Loosen the upholstery slightly.






4. Remove the bolts and pop rivet securing the lock.






5. Lift out the lock.

To fit

1. Lift the rear seat backrest lock into position.






2. Fit the lock bolts and pop rivet.
3. Fold back the upholster.






4. Fit Backrest lock plastic covers, 5D.
5. Raise the rear seat backrest and check the function of the lock.
