Convertible Top Hydraulic Hose: Description and Operation



Hydraulic Lines






The hydraulic lines are marked with the same number at both ends and are of different lengths. The passages through the attaching plate are also marked to avoid confusion.

It is essential the hoses are mounted correctly.
