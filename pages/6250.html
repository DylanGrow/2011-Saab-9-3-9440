To Fit, New Roof Rail, 5D



To Fit, New Roof Rail, 5D

To fit, new roof rail

1. Remove the roof moulding.
2. Remove the screws in the roof
3. Fit the seals on the roof rail.







4. Fit the clips on the roof rail.
5. Put in place the roof rail.
6. Fit the screws on the roof rail.

Tightening torque: 12 Nm (9 lbf ft)

7. Fit the plastic covers on the roof rail.
