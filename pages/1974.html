Rear Outer Side Cover, Electric Front Seat, CV



Rear Outer Side Cover, Electric Front Seat, CV

To remove

1. Move the seat to its highest position and remove the side panel with switch units.
2. Remove the screws and lift the seat upholstery.






To fit

1. Fit the side cover. Ensure that all hooks engage properly.






2. Fit the bolts.
3. Fit the side panel with switch units.
4. Reset the seat.
