Tipping Spring, Electric Front Seat, CV



Tipping Spring, Electric Front Seat, CV

To remove

1. Remove the backrest upholstery.
2. Remove the seat upholstery.
3. Remove belt reel inner cover.
4. Tip the backrest forward.
5. Remove the spring for tipping the backrest using a screwdriver.






To fit

1. Fold up the backrest.
2. Fit the spring for tipping the backrest using locking pliers and a screwdriver.






3. Fit the belt reel inner cover.
4. Fit the seat upholstery.
5. Fit the backrest upholstery.
