Cleaning Agent, Loctite Super Clean 7063



Cleaning Agent, Loctite Super Clean 7063






Specifications

Loctite Super Clean 7063. 150 ml spray can.

Range of application

Cleaning agent for most materials to be glued with Loctite anaerobic products, such as thread locker, thread sealant and products for mounting bushings.

Transport

The product is classified as dangerous goods when transporting. Special regulations apply to packaging, marking and transport, therefore existing lead times cannot be guaranteed.

Directives

The product is extremely flammable. Dehydrates the skin. May be hazardous if inhaled after frequent and repeated exposure. Pressurised can. Must not be exposed to direct sunlight or temperatures above +50 °C. Must not be punctured or burned. Also applies to empty can. Do not spray toward open flame or glowing materials. Must be stored separately from materials which may ignite the product - Smoking is prohibited. Avoid inhalation of spray mist. Make sure ventilation is adequate.
