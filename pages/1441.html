Visual Inspection of Seat Belt.



Visual Inspection Of Seat Belt.

1. Check the locking function.
2. Check the operation of the seat-belt warning lamp.
3. Check that the seat-belt strap is not damaged.
4. Check that the strap is not frayed by the locking tab or strap guide.
