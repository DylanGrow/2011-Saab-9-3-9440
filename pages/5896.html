Bootlid Hinges, CV



Bootlid Hinges, CV

To remove

1. Remove Bootlid gas cartridge spring, CV .
2. Note the hinge position at the front bolt. Remove the bolts. When removing the hinge, hold the bootlid with an assistant.






To fit

1. Move the hinge into the correct position and fit the bolts.






2. Fit Bootlid gas cartridge spring, CV .
3. Adjust the bootlid if necessary. See Bootlid adjustment, CV.
