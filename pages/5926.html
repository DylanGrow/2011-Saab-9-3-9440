Front Fender Liner: Service and Repair



Front Wing Liner

To remove

1. Remove the wheel.
2. Remove the wing liner nuts and bolts.







3. Remove the wing liner and any insulation.

To fit

1. Fit any insulation.
2. Position the wing liner against the wheel housing mountings.
3. Fit all nuts and bolts.







4. Fit the wheel.
