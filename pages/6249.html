Roof-Rack Mounting Sleeves



Roof-Rack Mounting Sleeves

To remove

1. Remove the roof moulding.
2. Unscrew the threaded sleeve.







To fit

1. Fit the sleeve.







2. Apply body sealer around the sleeve and leave to dry. Repair the paintwork as necessary.
3. Fit the roof moulding.
