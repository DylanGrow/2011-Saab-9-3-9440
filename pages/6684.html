Brake Light Switch (29), TCS/ESP

Brake light switch (29), TCS/ESP

Location

Brake light switch (29)

Main use

Brake light information is used by the control module to:

- Disengage the cruise control
- Limit fuel during idling
- Enable diagnosis of the vehicle speed signal.

Type

Closing contact.
