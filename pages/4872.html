Parking Brake Cable: Service and Repair

Handbrake cable

To remove

1. Remove the front side panel from the appropriate side of the floor console.





2. Remove the rear cover from the floor console.





3. Remove the rear side panel from the appropriate side of the floor console.
4. Release the Handbrake and remove the cable from the mechanism.





5. Remove the rear seat seat cushion.
6. Fold back the carpeting and remove the cable from the mounting in the floor.





7. Raise the car.
8. Remove the cable from the lever on the wheel cylinders and from the brackets.

Right-hand side: Remove the wiring harness.





9. Remove the rubber grommet and pull out the cable.

To fit

1. Thread through the cable and fit it to the brackets under the car.

Right-hand side: Fit the wiring harness.





2. Fit the rubber grommet.
3. Fit the cable to the wheel cylinder.
4. Lower the car.
5. Fit the cable to the bracket on the floor.





6. Fit the cable to the lever mechanism.





7. Put the floor carpet in place.
8. Fit the rear seat seat cushion.
9. Apply the Handbrake to tension the cable.

Release the Handbrake.

10. Raise the car.
11. Check and adjust the Handbrake cable.
12. Fit the rear side panel to the floor console.





13. Fit the rear cover to the floor console.
14. Fit the front side panel to the floor console.
