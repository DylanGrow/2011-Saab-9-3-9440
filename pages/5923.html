Moulding, Door Opening, 4D



Moulding, Door Opening, 4D

To remove

1. Loosen the strip using 82 93 474 Removal tool. Start at the front edge and lift the rear edge of the strip from the clip.







Refitting an old strip

1. Let the clips remain in the body. Replace clips as necessary.
2. Turn the clips so they are positioned correctly.







3. Fit the strip by guiding in its guide clips. Press in the strip so that the clips fasten.

Fitting a new strip

1. Remove the clips in the body using a knife.







IMPORTANT: Take care not to damage paintwork during removal.

2. Fit the strip by guiding in its guide clips. Press in the strip so that the clips fasten.
