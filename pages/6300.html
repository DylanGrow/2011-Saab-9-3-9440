Motor, Rake Adjustment, Electrically Operated Seat (357B), 4D/5D



Motor, Rake Adjustment, Electrically Operated Seat (357b), 4D/5D

Location

Motor, rake adjustment, electrically operated seat (357b).






Main task

To change the angle of the seat.

Type

DC motor that through a gear drives the mechanism that raises or lowers the seat frame at the front to change the angle of the seat.

Connect

The motor is connected to the switch unit pins 7 and 12. Depending on the switch's position, the motor is voltage fed and ground via the switch unit. The polarity of the outputs is reversed to change the motor's direction of rotation.
