Rear Bumper Shell Holder, CV



Rear Bumper Shell Holder, CV

Removing

1. Remove the Rear bumper shell, CV.
2. Remove the holder against the rear wing.







3. Remove the holder under the bootlid opening.
4. Remove the holder under the tail light.

Fitting

1. Fit the holder under the tail light.







2. Fit the holder under the bootlid.
3. Fit the holder to the rear wing.
4. Fit the Rear bumper shell, CV.
