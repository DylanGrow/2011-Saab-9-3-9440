Upholstery, Rear Seat Backrest, CV



Upholstery, Rear Seat Backrest, CV

To remove

1. Remove Rear seat backrest, CV.
2. Remove the upholstery and foam cushion from the rear section.






3. Cut the metal rings securing the upholstery.






4. Carefully pull the upholstery off the foam backrest cushion.

To fit

1. Fit the backrest upholstery over the foam cushion. Make sure the upholstery is properly stretched.
2. Fit the metal rings. Use the special tool 84 71 062 Fitting kit, upholstery.






3. Fit the upholstery and foam cushion to the rear section.






4. Fit Rear seat backrest, CV.
