Heating Pad, Front Seat Backrest



Heating Pad, Front Seat Backrest

Removing

1. Remove the seat.
2. Remove the backrest upholstery.

Fitting

1. Tape the new heating pad on top of the old one. Use double-sided adhesive tape. Plug in the heating pad connector.






2. Fit the backrest upholstery.
3. Fit the seat.
