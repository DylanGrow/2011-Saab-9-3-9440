Solenoid, Fuel Filler Flap, CV (434a)



Solenoid, Fuel Filler Flap, CV (434a)

To remove

1. Open the boot lid.
2. Remove Luggage compartment side trim, CV on the right hand side.
3. Loosen the solenoid retaining bolts and lift up the solenoid. Pull the rubber gaiter from the filler flap sleeve.






4. Part the connector.

To fit

Fit a new gaiter as follows:

1. Insert the gaiter through the filler flap sleeve hole using a pair of pliers.






2. Grip the solenoid's front gaiter and pull it out through the hole.






3. Place the solenoid in the mounting and tighten it. Connect the connector.






4. Cut off the end of the gaiter according to the markings.






5. Check the filler flap's locking function.
6. Fit Luggage compartment side trim, CV. Close the boot lid.
