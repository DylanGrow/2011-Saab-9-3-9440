Lumbar Support



Lumbar Support

Removing

1. Remove the seat.
2. Remove the front seat backrest upholstery.
3. Unhook the wires from their mountings by twisting the wires a quarter of a turn and then pulling them out.






4. Unhook the spring mat from its attachment points.
5. Unhook the lumbar support from its mountings.

Fitting

1. Hook the lumbar support to its mountings.






2. Hook the spring mat to its attachment points.
3. Attach the wires to their mountings. Make sure the wires are correctly seated in their guides.
4. Fit the backrest upholstery.
5. Fit the front seat.
