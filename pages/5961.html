Parcel Shelf Trim



Parcel Shelf Trim

To remove

1. Fold down the rear seat back rests.
2. Remove the clips securing the parcel shelf trim.







3. Pull the parcel shelf trim straight out forwards.

To fit

1. Slide in the parcel shelf trim, ensure that the locating lugs fully engage in their mountings on the parcel shelf panel.







2. Fit the parcel shelf trim clips.







3. Raise the backrest. Check that the back rests are locked.
