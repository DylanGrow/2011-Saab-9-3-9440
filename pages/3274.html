32 025 041 Silicone Grease



32 025 041 Silicone Grease






Specifications

Molykote PG21. Silicone grease for long-term lubrication of plastic/metal, plastic/plastic and rubber/metal combinations. Temperature -50 °C to +190 °C. 10 g tube.

Range of application

Distribution and heating vent in heating and ventilation unit. Anti-roll bar bushings.
