Front Door Handle



Front Door Handle

To remove

1. Driver's door: Remove the bolt (A).






2. Passenger door: Remove the cover (B).
3. Hold the opening handle (C) in its outer position and unscrew the bolt (D) to the stop position.
4. Remove the handle by:
- Loosen the rear part (E) of the handle.






- Remove the front part (F) by moving it rearwards and unhooking the front edge of the handle.

To fit

1. Fit the door handle in place.
- Fit the front part (F) by hooking the front edge of the handle into place.






- Fit the rear part (E) of the handle.

2. Hold the opening handle (C) in its outer position and screw in the bolt (D) to the stop position.






3. Driver's door: Fit the bolt (A)
4. Passenger door: Fit the cover (B)
