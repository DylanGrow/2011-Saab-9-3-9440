Weatherstrip, Boot Lid



Weatherstrip, Boot Lid

To remove

1. Open the boot lid and remove the weatherstrip by pulling it straight out.







2. Shut the boot lid slightly and slide the weatherstrip off over the boot lid.

To fit

1. Shut the boot lid slightly and slide the weatherstrip off over the boot lid.







2. Fit the weatherstrip. Use a rubber hammer so that the weatherstrip is properly located.
