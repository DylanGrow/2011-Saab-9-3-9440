Clip For Clutch Pedal Spring

Clip for clutch pedal spring

To remove

1. Press the clutch pedal forward.
2. Remove (lift off) the clutch spring from the clutch pedal.





3. Remove the clip (if it is still in place).

To fit

1. Fit the clip.





2. Move the clutch pedal spring up and position it in the clip.
3. Move the clutch pedal forward and hook the clutch pedal spring into place.
