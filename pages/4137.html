Rocker Arm Assembly: Service and Repair

Rocker arm, camshaft

To remove

1. Remove the camshafts.
2. Lift off the rocker arms (A).





To fit

Important
The camshafts must be replaced when replacing the rocker arms

1. Fit the rocker arms (A). Dip the rocker arms in oil before fitting. Make sure that the rocker arms make contact with the valve stem and tappet.
2. Fit the camshafts.
