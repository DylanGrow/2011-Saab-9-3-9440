Trim And Warranty

Saab dealers offer a wide selection of Saab original trim and styling products for your vehicle within the scope of the Saab spare part and accessories programme. All Saab spare parts and accessories are covered by the Saab spare part warranty.

The use of trim and styling products which are not approved and sold by Saab Automobile AB will invalidate the Saab warranty with respect to all affected technical systems and in other respects as defined in the warranty for your particular market. Saab Automobile AB will not accept any liability, as a manufacturer or as a warranty agent, for faults which are caused by trim and styling products which are not approved and sold by Saab Automobile AB.
