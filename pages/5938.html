Side Grille



Side Grille

To remove

1. Remove the front bumper shell.
2. Detach the grille.
- Bend out the locking catches (A) from the inside of the shell using a screwdriver to release the catches from the bumper shell. Continue around the entire grille until all the catches have released.







3. Remove the grille.

To fit

1. Guide in the grille and press in all catches (A) so that they engage.







2. Fit the front bumper shell.
