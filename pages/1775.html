Door Mirror Frame



Door Mirror Frame

To remove

1. Fold the door mirror forward.
2. Angle out the glass.
3. Unhook the spring clip using a screwdriver.






4. Unplug the connector and lift aside the glass.






5. Press in the clips on the cover to release it. Start with the outer clips.

Fold in the mirror and pull off the cover.

6. Remove the screws securing the motor.
7. Lift out the motor and unplug the connector.






8. Remove the screws securing the frame.






9. Bend aside the plastic catch to release the frame.

To fit

1. Fit the frame so that the clip engages.
2. Fit the screws securing the frame.






3. Plug in the connector.






4. Position the motor.
5. Fit the screws securing the motor.






6. Press the cover home.

Fold the door mirror forward and check that the clips have engaged in their holes.

7. Make sure that the spring clips on the glass are correctly positioned.
8. Plug in the mirror connector.






9. Fit the glass to the motor.
10. Carefully press in the glass until you hear a click.
11. Check the function of the door mirror.
