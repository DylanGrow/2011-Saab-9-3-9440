Rear Seat Backrest Lock, 4D



Rear Seat Backrest Lock, 4D

Removing

1. Tip the rear seat backrest forward.
2. Remove the parcel shelf trim.
3. Remove the backrest lock handle by carefully pressing together the catches. Pull the handle downward and rearward.






4. Remove the clip securing the cable from the lock handle to the lock.






5. Remove the bolts securing the rear seat backrest lock and lift up the lock.
6. Unplug the connector from the lock.
7. Lift out the lock.

Fitting

1. Lift the rear seat backrest lock into position.
2. Plug in the connector.
3. Fit the bolts securing the rear seat backrest lock.






Tightening torque 26 Nm (20 lbf ft)

4. Fit the clip securing the cable from the lock handle to the rear seat backrest lock.
5. Fit the lock handle to the underside of the parcel shelf. Make sure the catches engage.






6. Fit the parcel shelf trim.
7. Raise the rear seat backrest and check the function of the lock.
