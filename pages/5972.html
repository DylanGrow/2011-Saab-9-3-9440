A-Pillar Trim



A-pillar Trim

To remove

1. Remove the screw cover from the A-pillar trim. Use 82 93 474 Removal tool.







2. Remove the retaining bolts from the A-pillar trim.
3. Grip the bottom edge of the A-pillar trim and carefully pull straight out.

To fit

1. Fit the A-pillar trim into its position.

WARNING: Take care not to damage the inflatable curtain. Any damage can prevent the inflatable curtain from providing the intended protection for passengers in the event of a collision.

2. Fit the A-pillar trim fixing screws.







Tightening torque 5 Nm (4 lbf ft)

3. Fit the screw cover to the A-pillar trim.
