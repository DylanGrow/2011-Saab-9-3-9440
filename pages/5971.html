Sun Visor, CV



Sun Visor, CV

To remove

1. Pull the sun visor from the inner holder.
2. Remove the inner holder for the sun visor.







3. Remove the outer holder.
4. Remove the sun visor connectors.







To fit

1. Connect the sun visor connectors.







2. Fit the sun visor outer bracket.

NOTE: Make sure that the leads are not pinched.







3. Fit the inner holder for the sun visor.
4. Press the sun visor securely into the inner bracket.
