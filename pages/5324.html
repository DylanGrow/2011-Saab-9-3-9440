Blower Motor Switch: Service and Repair



Ventilation Fan Control

To remove

1. Put the seat in its rearmost position.
2. Remove the glove box.
3. Remove the passenger acoustic insulation
4. Dismantle the connector from the ventilation fan control.






5. Dismantle the ventilation fan control's securing screws.
6. Dismantle the ventilation fan control by pulling it outwards and back.

To fit

1. Fit the ventilation fan control.






2. Connect the ventilation fan control's connector.
3. Fit the passenger acoustic insulation.
4. Refit the glove box.
