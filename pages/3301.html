93 165 228 Ultra Fast Adhesive



93 165 228 Ultra Fast Adhesive






Specifications

AK 8200. Single component adhesive based on cyanoacrylate acid esters. 10 g bottle.

Saab application

Adhesive for timing cover, V6 Saab 900/9000.

Packaging

1

Transport

No restrictions apply when transporting this product.

Directives

Cyanoacrylic. Warning! Can quickly glue together eyes and skin. Avoid contact with the skin and eyes. Do not inhale. In case of contact with the eyes, rinse immediately with plenty of water and seek medical help.
