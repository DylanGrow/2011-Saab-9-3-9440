Cylindrical Fastening, Loctite 648



Cylindrical Fastening, Loctite 648

Specifications

Loctite 648. Locking fluid, high temperature. Strong.

NOTE: Must not be used together with aluminium. 50 ml bottle.

Range of application

Splines front wheel hub Saab 9000. Thread locking compound for lock nut on universal joint Saab 9000 and Saab 99/90/900 -M93 pinion nut, outer universal joint.

Transport

No restrictions apply when transporting this product.

Directives

The product is an irritant. May irritate the eyes and skin. In case of splash in eyes, flush with plenty of water and contact a physician. In case of skin contact, wash immediately with soap and water.

Use Loctite dosage equipment in order to avoid contact with eyes and skin.
