Noise From Front Assembly

Noise from front assembly

Fault symptom

A disruptive noise which is coming from the front assembly.

Conditions

If a bolt connection has settled, noise can occur when the upper and lower nuts on the anti-roll bar link lose tightening torque.

Procedure

Check that the tightening torque is correct. Tighten both nuts.

Tightening torque 64 Nm (47 lbf ft)
