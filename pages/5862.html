Rear Door, Opening Cable



Rear Door, Opening Cable

To remove

1. Remove Rear door trim.
2. Carefully remove the water barrier from the door.
3. Remove the opening cable.
- Undo the clips (A).






- Detach the opening handle's rod (B) from the clip (C) in the door lock.
- Remove the door lock's screws (A).






- Push the door lock forward until the cable can be removed.






- Remove the cable (A) from the door lock.






To fit

1. Fit the opening cable.
- Fit the cable (A) to the door lock.






- Position the lock. Fit the lock's screws (A).






- Fit the rod (B) in the clip (C). Close the lock cover for the clip.






- Fit the clips (A).

2. Fit the plastic water barrier.
3. Fit Rear door trim.
