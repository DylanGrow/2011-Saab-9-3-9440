Air Vent, Panel, Centre



Air Vent, Panel, Centre

To remove

1. Remove the centre air vent as follows:

IMPORTANT: To prevent the vents from breaking, never pull the adjusting buttons. Make sure that the correct catches are bent in. The catches which hold together the vents can also be seen through the grille.







- Carefully press in 82 93 474 Removal tool between panel and air vent, so that the air vent is tensioned.
- Carefully prize up one of the air grille's clips by inserting a small screwdriver down into the fourth hole from the right, as far as possible (approx. 20 mm) (A). Carefully prize down the screwdriver while at the same time maintaining the pressure with the removal tool (B).







- When the air vent has loosened slightly, insert the screwdriver to detach the second clip in the air duct's ninth hole from the right (C). Carefully prize in the screwdriver while at the same time maintaining the pressure with the removal tool (D).







- Reposition the removal tool and press it into the top edge (E) so that the upper clip releases.

Grip the edges of the air vent and detach the third lower clip by inserting the screwdriver into the fourth hole from the left (F). Prize down the screwdriver and at the same time pull out the air vent.







To fit

1. Position and fit the centre panel vent (A). Make sure that the catches fasten securely.
