Side Window, Rear Door



Side Window, Rear Door

To remove

1. Open the window fully.
2. Remove The door trim, rear and fold down the water barrier.
3. Remove the window frame cover.
4. Remove the outer and inner weatherstrips using 82 93 474 Removal tool.






5. Remove the U-moulding from the forward glass channel.






6. Remove the screws from the forward glass channel.
7. Remove the forward side moulding.
8. Lift away the forward glass channel.
9. Raise the window in order to gain access to the window bolts. Remove the bolts






10. Lift out the window.

To fit

1. Fit the side window by angling it in towards the rear U-moulding and press it down on the mountings.

Make sure the window is touching the rear U-moulding. Fit the window bolts.






2. Open the window fully.
3. Fit the front glass channel.






4. Fit the front side moulding. Make sure that the lower part fits snugly against the door side moulding.
5. Raise the window approx. 5 cm so that you can move the window slightly.
6. Insert the front U-moulding a way into the glass channel. Help by pressing the window slightly backward. Lower the window fully and fit the rest of the moulding. Press the moulding into the glass channel. Check the function of the window.
7. Fit the outer and inner weatherstrip. Raise and lower the window a few times to check its operation.






8. Fit the water barrier and Door trim, rear.
9. Fit the window frame cover.

Cars with pinch protection: Perform Calibration of pinch protection.
