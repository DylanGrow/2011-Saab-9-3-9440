Rear Lower Cover, Front Seat, CV



Rear Lower Cover, Front Seat, CV

To remove

1. Move the seat to its highest position and fold down the backrest.
2. Remove the rivets for the rear cover using a screwdriver.






3. Lift the cover up.

To fit

1. Position the cover.
2. Fit the rivets.






3. Reset the seat.
