Grey Dirt Stains on the Outer Roof



Grey Dirt Stains On The Outer Roof

Background

Grey dirt stains may appear on the outer roof. The reason could be that there is hard pressure on the soft top when it is operated. Careful cleaning can minimise these stains.

Fault symptoms

Grey dirt stains on the outer roof.

Procedure

1. Clean in accordance with Cleaning the outer roof, soft top.
