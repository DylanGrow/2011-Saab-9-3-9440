Window Lift Motor, Rear, CV



Window Lift Motor, Rear, CV

To remove

1. Remove Backrest, rear seat, CV, Seat cushion, rear seat, CV and Rear side trim, CV.
2. Remove the water barrier.






3. Remove the screws from the window lift motor.
4. Lift out the window lift motor. Unplug the connector.

To fit

1. Plug in the connector. Fit the window lift motor. Align the motor with the help of the guide pins.






2. Fit the window lift motor screws.
3. Fit the water barrier.
4. Fit Rear side trim, Backrest, rear seat, CV, Seat cushion, rear seat, CV.
5. Cars with pinch protection: Perform Calibration of pinch protection.
