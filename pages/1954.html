Backrest Tab, Front Seat, CV



Backrest Tab, Front Seat, CV

To remove

1. Fold the backrest down and remove the backrest tab clips using 82 93 474 Removal tool.






2. Lift up the tab so that the hooks in the upper edge detach from the cover.






To fit

1. Attach the hooks to the cover.
2. Press the clips in place.
3. Fold up the backrest.
