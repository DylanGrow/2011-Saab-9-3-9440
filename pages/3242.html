Primer, Etching, Standox 1K-Fullprimer



Primer, Etching, Standox 1K-Fullprimer






Specifications

Standox 1K-Fullprimer. Acid-hardening. For normal and galvanised sheet metal. 1 litre can.

Range of application

Transport

The product is classified as dangerous goods when transporting. Special regulations apply to packaging, marking and transport, therefore existing lead times cannot be guaranteed.

Directives

The product is flammable. May be hazardous if inhaled after frequent and repeated exposure. Dehydrates the skin. Avoid inhaling vapours/mist. Avoid skin contact. Make sure that ventilation is adequate.
