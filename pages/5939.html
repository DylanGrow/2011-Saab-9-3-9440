Arm Rest: Service and Repair



Armrest, Centre Console

To remove

1. Remove the Rear panel vent.
2. Fold up the armrest.







3. Remove the armrest hinge using 82 93 474 Removal tool.

To fit

1. Fit the armrest hinge in the centre console and press in the armrest.







2. Fit the Rear panel vent.
