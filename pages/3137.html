Anti-Seize Lubricant, Gleitmo 980



Anti-Seize Lubricant, Gleitmo 980






Specifications

Gleitmo 980. For metal, plastic, rubber, wood, leather, paper board and ceramics (dry lubricant). Good lubricating and anti-seize properties. 400 ml spray can.

Saab application

Plastic parts in gear-lever housing Saab 900/9000.

Transport

The product is classified as dangerous goods when transporting. Special regulations apply to packaging, marking and transport, therefore existing lead times cannot be guaranteed.

Directives

The product is extremely flammable. May be hazardous if inhaled after frequent and repeated exposure. May be hazardous in case of prolonged skin contact. Pressurized can. Must not be exposed to direct sunlight or temperatures above +50 °C. Must not be punctured or burned. Also applies to empty can. Do not spray toward open flame or glowing materials. Must be stored separately from materials which may ignite the product - Smoking is prohibited. Make sure that ventilation is adequate. Avoid inhaling spray mist. Avoid eye and skin contact.
